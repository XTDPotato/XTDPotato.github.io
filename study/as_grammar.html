"as" 连词的不同用法及其对应的状语从句类型

用法 状语从句类型 例句
让步状语从句 虽然/尽管 As wealthy as he is, he lives a simple life.
即使/纵然 As busy as you are, please try to make time to attend my wedding.
时间状语从句 同时/正在 As we were walking, we saw a beautiful sunset.
一边...一边... As he was eating, he was reading a book.
原因状语从句 因为/既然 He failed the exam as he didn't study enough.
由于/鉴于 As she was feeling sick, she decided to stay home.

这是 "as" 连词的不同用法及其对应的状语从句类型。
